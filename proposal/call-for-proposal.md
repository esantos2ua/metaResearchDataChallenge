# Call for Proposal — Canadian Metaresearch Data Challenge

Source: <https://carn-recar.ca/2nd-canadian-open-science-conference-call-for-proposal-canadian-metaresearch-data-challenge/>
Part of the **2nd Canadian Open Science Conference**, University of Ottawa.

## Key question

> How can we construct an open, reproducible, and inclusive dataset that best captures the
> Canadian metaresearch landscape?

## Objective

Develop an approach for mapping the Canadian metaresearch landscape that is open, reproducible,
and inclusive of diverse research traditions and methodological approaches.

## Eligible participants

Researchers & trainees · librarians & information professionals · infrastructure/platform
specialists · data scientists & developers · open science practitioners · interdisciplinary teams.
All career stages and nationalities welcome; teams need not be Canada-based. Individual or team
submissions accepted.

## Research areas of interest

Metascience/metaresearch · bibliometrics & scientometrics · science & technology studies (STS) ·
research integrity & reproducibility · scholarly communication research · open science/scholarship.

## Suggested proposal components

- Search & retrieval strategies (using OpenAlex)
- Inclusion/exclusion criteria for community definition
- Validation and bias assessment plans
- Documentation and reproducibility practices
- Topic modeling and thematic mapping
- Citation and bibliometric analyses
- Network analyses (co-authorship, institutional collaboration)
- Open dissemination strategies
- Methods for identifying underrepresented groups

## Submission requirements

- Complete application form via the conference website
- **2-page maximum** methodology description addressing:
  - Openness, transparency, and reproducibility measures
  - Diversity considerations and bias acknowledgment
  - Work plan for pre-conference completion

## Key dates

| Milestone | Date |
|-----------|------|
| Submission deadline | **July 1, 2026** |
| Notification | July 10, 2026 |

## Evaluation criteria

Methodological rigor · timeline feasibility · reproducibility & transparency · inclusiveness &
diversity · originality & potential impact · clarity of outputs & documentation.

## Deliverables (selected teams)

- Implement methodology before the conference
- Produce an open dataset with documentation
- Share code and workflows where possible
- Present findings in a **30-minute plenary session**

## Support

Funding up to **CAD $4,000** for selected teams (travel, accommodation, participation).
